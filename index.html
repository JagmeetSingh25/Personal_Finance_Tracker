Welcome to the Finance Tracker App, where we aim to manage your money and try to make it simpler and more efficient to use!

Our Purpose

At Finance Tracker, our purpose is to empower individuals to take control of their finances by providing tools and insights to effectively manage income, expenses, and savings.

Features

  • Expense Tracking: Easily record and categorize your expenses.
  • Income Log: Keep track of your sources of income.
  • Savings Goals: Set and monitor your financial goals.
